I grew up between Mumbai, India and Brussels, Belgium, which gave me a deep appreciation for different cultures and ways of thinking.

I'm currently studying Computer Science and Mathematics at Duke University. I'm especially interested in the overlap between machine learning, software engineering, and robotics (with a focus on Embodied AI). I am super excited about working on projects with real-world impact.

Whether it's building out full-stack applications, training models, or experimenting with autonomous systems, I’m always curious and excited to learn something new.

My Interests

Taekwondo

I'm a member of the Duke Taekwondo Team and have competed in several national championships. I also help organize events and support the club's community.

I started Taekwondo after coming to Duke and both the sport and the community have since become very important parts of my life.

Last year, I earned a bronze medal at the Eastern Collegiate Taekwondo Championships hosted by Brown University.

eNable

eNable is a global, decentralized organization that 3D prints prosthetic limbs for children in need.

I founded and led the Belgian branch of eNable, which became one of the most meaningful and impactful experiences of my life.

I'm still involved with the organization and currently work on developing improved control systems for 3D-printed prosthetics at Duke.

Fantasy Books

I enjoy reading books, and am drawn to complex world-building and power systems. Recently, I have been very into Brandon Sanderson and the Stormlight Archive series.

Music Composition

I enjoy composing and producing music in Logic and Ableton. I played violin for over 10 years, but recently I’ve found greater creativity and joy in creating my own music from scratch.
